About Me

I am Ye Dong (董业), a Ph.D. student at IIE, CAS under Prof. Xiaojun Chen. I mainly focus on privacy-preserving machine learning, including secure multi-party computation, homomorphic encryption, distributed machine learning, and federated learning. I got my bachelor degree in School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University. I had been one Research Assistant at NETWORK & INFORMATION SECURITY LAB under Prof. Quiliang Xu.
